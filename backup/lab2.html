Geography

Chicago is in Illinois in the Midwest. It is considered the crown jewel of its state region. For those that are not familiar with the geography of the United States, please click here!

Located on the southwestern tip of Lake Michign, one of the Great Lakes, Chicago was central to westward expansion in the early years of US history. In fact, Upton Sinclair's classic novel "The Jungle" chronicles the experience of Chicagoans during the Industrial Revolution.
